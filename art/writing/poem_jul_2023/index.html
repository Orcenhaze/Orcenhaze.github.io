Untitled

Poem - Jul. 2023

Aid me through the tiring phase
Of hefty weight and dusky haze

Aid me with your calm embrace
Sleep therein and rest for days

Sing a song to remind us all
What made the Romans slowly fall

Make me stand against its face
And grant the snake a deadly gaze
