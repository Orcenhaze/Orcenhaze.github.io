Thither

Poem - 2018

From the ashes buried deep
In the ground far below
You get a glimpse of eyes in creep
As you’re watching in the snow
Then the fire bursts to weep
All those faces who keep their senses stare too long

In this world that’s underground
What was it created for?
What would we there have found?
What would the devil have in store?

From the souls flying high
To the sky far above
You see a hand reaching out
Calling you to join in of
All the fountains full of wine
All the things there are to love

In that world that’s overhead
Will we see the light of day?
What’s the place we’ll be a part?
End in heaven and forever stay
